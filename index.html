Online Chronometer

A chronometer is a timepiece or instrument for measuring time intervals very accurately. This online chronometer allows you to measure time intervals with precision.

To use the chronometer:

  1. Click on the "Start" button to begin timing.
  2. Click on the "Record" button to record laps (times) while the chronometer is running.
  3. Click on the "Reset" button to reset the chronometer and clear all recorded laps.
